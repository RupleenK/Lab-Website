Isaac is a second-year Master's student in Systems Biology and Bioinformatics at Case Western Reserve University. He graduated from Harbin University of Science and Technology in China with a Bachelor's in Applied Mathematics. His research interest is mathematical modeling and machine learning for medical image analysis. In the Dhawan lab, he focuses on developing PDE–based models to characterize and predict the growth dynamics of meningiomas using longitudinal MRI data.
